Game2Learn is a gamified learning solution integrated into customer training, aimed to replace traditional course-end assessments, and to serve as a retention tool for learners long after class is over.

The playful component of gamification, along with analytics and leaderboard features, allows learners and their managers to have a clear view of competence gaps at a targeted granularity level.
